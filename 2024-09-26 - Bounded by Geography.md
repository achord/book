# Bounded by Geography = Bounded by Opportunity

What are are the chance that the love of your life, your dream job, beauty, inspiration, etc all exist with in a five mile radius?  If you're lucky enough to live in a locale with natural and economic resources, and can afford to live, congratulations.  Most of us have to move.

A limiting belief held by many is that it won't be better anywhere else, so why bother. 
### Bother because
- When you go where the action is, opportunites arise that simple don't come byb being remote
- You open yourself up to being a part of scene. Be it music, art, or technology, rising tides raise all ships. Be amongst the best in your field of interest. Feed of the energy and grow exponentially.
- Adventure
- Access to Nature
- Access to beauty
- Access to potential mates
- Access to inspiration
