### Why am I writing this?
I turned forty-seven and Time flipped a switch. I realized I was in decline for the first time in my life. My health, my body, my opportunities. Every moment before seemed like another rung higher on the ladder. I never felt the pressure of time.

Why 47? For the past 4 years, life has been hard. We had baby Sam, bought a house. The house was soon damaged by Hurricane Ida. We were displaced for almost two years. My father fell ill and was in and out of hospitals. My Mom was diagnosed with COPD, then a slow terrible decline until her death in August of 2022. I was let go from my job. And a host of other, normal life problems. Money issues, a car wreck, crazy stress. in 2023-24 Cort had one of the worst years as a Teacher than a person can have.

Any now I find myself, for the first time in 4 years, with a moment to reflect. To maybe tie the loose ends of the chaotic recent past. And in this clearing I;m realizing how much knowledge I have in my brain. The books, the lessons learned the hard way. I was never given a foundation on which to build my life from my parents.  I never felt that I lacked love or resources, but now, wth an older perspective, I'm realize that it's taken me a lifetime to figure some things out. Finances for example. Had I been given any advice or mentorship from my Dad, I may be in a better place now.  I want the young people in my life, especislly the young men, to at least have a foundation on which to build a life of autonomy and purpose.  If my suspicions are correct, they will likely, act as youth, and make their own mistakes. But hopefully, some of the principles I've laid out will guide them.

We, currently, seem to be under a false sense of enlightenment.  The information age has provided us with the answers to most of life's problems. (give examples) so we think, "If I need to know X, and I can just look it up". But this is far different from crafting as life for yourself.  And the real challenge is that the world, in America at least, is changing at a blistering (exponential) pace. I hope I've addressed foundation human needs, as well as provided a framework to evolve with the pace of a modern economy.

I do not know what will happen to me. If I get hit by a bus and want Sam to know how t navigate this insane world. When men lose their male role models, the become at risk for all sorts of bad things. I want to build a foundation for the young men in my life and provide them with the tools to build a life.

## Notes on Giving Advice:

https://fs.blog/hunter-s-thompson-to-hume-logan/

An opinion is something given when asked.
And opinion given when not asked is (interjectory) rude.


"You ask advice: ah, what a very human and very dangerous thing to do! For to give advice to a man who asks what to do with his life implies something very close to egomania. To presume to point a man to the right and ultimate goal— to point with a trembling finger in the RIGHT direction is something only a fool would take upon himself."
Hunter S. Thompson

I offer up this information, it's your job to apply critical thinking. To cull (gather) the parts that speak to you, and ignore the rest.
