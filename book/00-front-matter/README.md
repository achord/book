## Chapter Purpose

This chapter contains the author's foreword and framing letters. The material establishes why the book was written (a mid-life reckoning at age 47, after Hurricane Ida, loss of a parent, job loss), who it is for (young men, middle class, introverts, those without strong male role models), and the book's four core principles: **Autonomy**, **Consistency**, **Connection**, and **Time**.
