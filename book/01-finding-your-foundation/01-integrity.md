# Integrity

Become a man who lives by integrity.
- Make decisions with integrity
- Lead with honesty, not rudeness (another chapter), but be honest, even when it's uncomfortable
- Set high-standards for yourself
- Define your core values
  E.g.
  - Authenticity
  - Autonomy
  - Beauty
  - Compassion
  - Competence
  - Creativity
  - Curiosity
  - Determination
  - Empathy
  - Focus
  - Fun
  - Hard work
  - Kindness
  - Learning
  - Optimism
  - Passion
  - Playfulness
  - Pleasure
  - Respect
  - Tenacity
  - Trustworthiness
