# Chapter 1: Finding Your Foundation

## Chapter Purpose

This chapter is about self-knowledge — who you are, what you stand for, and how you want to live. The author's argument is that a man must consciously define his identity and values, or others will define it for him. Integrity, core values, and the willingness to act rightly regardless of outcome are the foundation everything else rests on.
