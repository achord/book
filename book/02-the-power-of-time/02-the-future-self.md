# The Future Self

## July 29, 2025

Exercise

- Have a conversation with your future self - use AI
- The trick to the whole thing is you need to pull back and think about the scale of your life
  - Your place in the timeline and
  - How do you want the future to be written
  - How do you want the story to end
  - What do you want the middle to be like

- Envision the future
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XyhhwVJB9Z4&t=817s
- Envision yourself in 5, 10, 15, 20 years. What kind of life do you want to live? What kind of person do you want to be?
  - figure out where you want to be
  - Work it backwards
  - break it down into skills
  - Learn those skills
  - ^ stack the odds in your favor to make the above happen
