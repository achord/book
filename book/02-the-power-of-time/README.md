# Chapter 2: The Power of Time

## Chapter Purpose

Youth is the most underrated asset most people possess. This chapter argues that the ability to think on a macro scale — to see your life as a whole arc from birth to death — is the key distinguishing trait between people who build meaningful lives and those who don't. Time compounds: money, skills, and character all grow with consistent effort over time. The cost of delay is real and quantifiable.
