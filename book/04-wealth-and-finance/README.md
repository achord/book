# Chapter 4: Wealth and Finance

## Chapter Purpose

Financial literacy is foundational. The author's argument: most people are never taught how money works, and compound interest — understood early — is the single most powerful tool available to a young person. Delayed gratification, saving, and avoiding the major financial traps (credit card debt, lifestyle inflation, big purchases that consume autonomy) are the core messages. Financial freedom is framed as a mindset and a form of independence, not a specific dollar amount.
