## November 6, 2025 — Notes on Work and Design Leadership

A leadership team that understands:
- The importance of design
- Gives time and space for great design to happen
- Design takes time
- Design in non-linear
- Spends money on typography
- That great work is iterative. Take off the blinders. Work, features, layouts, flows etc are not finite. No long conversations about speculation... I think.... Gather enough feedback, build a thing, measure, learn, iterate

## October 2025 — Scott Galloway on Career

- Don't follow your passion, follow your talent (then pursue mastery)
- Work-shopping in your 20's. Find what you're good at.
  - Book: Design Your Life
- Go to work to
 - Put money in your pocket
 - Increase your currency (value) in the marketplace
 - make connections: friends, mentors, co-founders, etc
- Move to a city: NY, San Fran
- If it's not a Hell Yes!, its a No.
- MUST HAVE SKILLS (Timeless skills)
  - Learn how to communicate
  - Learn how to get along with people that you disagree with
- Build your foundation
  - Integrity, Be a life-long learner, define your convictions (there will be a test)
