# Chapter 5: Career and Work

## Chapter Purpose

Work is where autonomy is earned or surrendered. This chapter covers the author's notes on what it means to build a career rather than just hold a job, how to show up and produce value, and the importance of choosing work that aligns with talent (not just passion). Notes from design practice, Scott Galloway's career framework, and the signal-vs-noise productivity model are the main source materials.
