# Chapter 6: Connection and Relationships

## Chapter Purpose

Human connection is the bedrock of happiness, opportunity, and creative work. The author argues that isolation kills creativity and potential, that geography constrains opportunity, that travel builds empathy, and that communication is the single most important skill most young men are never taught. This chapter collects the author's notes on these interlocking themes.
