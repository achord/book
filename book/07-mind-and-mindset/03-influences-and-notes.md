## October 2025 — Scott Galloway

### Scott Galloway
- Don't follow your passion, follow your talent (then pursue mastery)
- Work-shopping in your 20's. Find what you're good at.
  - Book: Design Your Life
- Go to work to
 - Put money in your pocket
 - Increase your currency (value) in the marketplace
 - make connections: friends, mentors, co-founders, etc
- You can not control things, but you can control how you react
- It's ok to not be ok
  - Reach out for help if a failure is creeping into atrophy
  - Mourn and move on
- Don't go it alone
- Move to a city: NY, San Fran
- If it's not a Hell Yes!, its a No.
- [Bring Solutions](../../source-archive/2024-09-26%20-%20Bring%20Solutions.md)
- Bounded by Geography = Bounded by Opportunity
- Avoid Stupidity
  - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zV5QUlUlzrk
  - You don't have to be a genius, but avoiding obvious mistakes is easy.
- Your ability to thrive may depend on how quick you can learn and adapt. And Leraning nad adapting will depend on how focused you are, and how focused you are will rely on your ability to KEEP THINGS SIMPLE. Be an absolute beast, be unappologetic about your ability to say NO. Destroy clutter in your life. Do not focus on too many things. let others do the grunt work of chasing trends. focus on what moves the needle, but above all else, give your mind the gift of space.  If you can focus on a few things that matter vs 100 things that sort of matter, you will go very far.  You will feel like you can bench press the world, because you kept it simple. (Give two real world examples)
- Depression: It is normal. It is ok to not be ok. Depression is not a choice. Time will heal you.  get past a rough moment. "Depression is not necessarily pathological. It often foreshadows a renewal of the personality or a burst of creative activity. There are moments in human life when a new page is turned." - Carl Jung
- Live with your whole heart. There will be haters. Do it anyway
- It's never too late to try again. It doesn't matter if you have failed. You get to decide if this experience breaks you, or makes you into a better person. You get to decide how the story is written.
- The power of thinking long-term and being able to see trends, markets, your life, at a macro scale
- Love and Relationships
  - Red Flags look like Yellow flags
- On Status and relationships. status gives you opportunity
  - But be aware about showing people what you have, you may not need it (e.g. mansion)
- MUST HAVE SKILLS (Timeless skills)
  - Learn how to communicate
  - Learn how to get along with people that you disagree with
- On Vulnerability
  - Foster connection and trust
  - Rush: Freewill "If you choose not to decide, You have made a choice"
- Build your foundation
  - Integrity, Be a life-long learner, define your convictions (there will be a test)

## August 2025 — Daniel Pink

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w39A92UzTDY

- Don't build a resume, build a body of work
 - Show your work
- Be persistent
- Action beats planning
- Be open to new ideas and perspectives
- Get moving, body moving
- You will regret what you didn't do
- Hofstadter's Law: It will always take longer than you think
- Ask for advice
  - People want to help
- Make things as simple as possible
- Luck matters, put yourself in the path by working hard
- Don't/Stop waiting for permission
- Protect your reputation
- Focus on what you can control, not what you can't
- Consistency wins the day, the week, the year, the life
- Treat everyone with respect. You never know who will move within the social structure
- Fight mediocrity
- Always Ask
  - The answer is always no if you don't ask
  - Most people want to help
  - Often asking others helps them break out of the boring
  - This is not to say ask instead of doing it yourself
- Be curious
- No one is coming to save you. It's on you.
- The most important decision is who you spend time with
  - Spouse
  - Friends
  - Work
  - Etc
- Take breaks
  - working out
  - Vacations
- Leave beneath means
  - Spend to: save time, invest, education, new experiences, connection

- Happiness comes from: meaning, contribution, growth, love
