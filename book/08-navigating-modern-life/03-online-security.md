# Online Security

## June 7, 2024

### Needs to be paraphrased
Social Engineers may use public information to create tailored attacks. They also have the ability to build sites that look identical to the real thing to trick unsuspecting targets into relinquishing credentials. It's important to use caution when providing credentials or payment information to unfamiliar sites or platforms. It is best practice to double-check URLs, remain suspicious of links and navigate directly to secure sites whenever in doubt.
