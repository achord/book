# Communication is key
- Learn to be a great communicator, both online and in person. Never leave the other person guessing. 
- Don't hide in the shadows, operate with transparency, and speak on uncomfortable topis, sooner rather than later. Late on the project? Speak up now. Not really into the person you've been dating for three months? tell them before it becomes three years. Speak up. Say something. Don't worry too much about the specifics, just gte the key points across
- The curse of so many young people, especially young men, is they were never taught (forced) to communicated. Being raised on video games and television hasn't helped either. I've had the displeasure of working with such people, and it's miserable.  When project get cut, and people have to be laid-off, rest assured it will be the non-communicators who go first.
